About Me

despite my young age. One of the downs I had in my life, that I currently and forever will
regret was my poor decision on not finishing up High School. I gave up during half way into

into a slump. At that time, I my lazyness take over and I gave myself endless excues to give up.
Thankfully I was able to push myself out of that slumb a year later.



Now I am a Realtor at Cannan Realty and a Project Manager/Owner of a small start up construction business,
Jiang's Construction Services with my father with over 15 years of construction experience.
